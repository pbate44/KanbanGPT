Our story

Built to help you think

KanbanGPT is a focused, AI-powered Kanban note taking app built for people who want clarity, momentum, and a calm place to collect and analyze information.

Origin

Why we built it

KanbanGPT has been designed to be an intuitive and easy to use note taking app.

The AI layer helps you think and execute faster — extracting key information, summarising work, and keeping you focused on what actually matters.

KanbanGPT was built by a solo developer. The core idea: an app to keep notes on different tasks in a clear kanban format, with an AI assistant that can help extract information and create new ideas.

Our philosophy

Simple workflows win. The best tool is the one you keep using.
