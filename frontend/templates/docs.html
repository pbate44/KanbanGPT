How KanbanGPT works

Everything you need to get the most out of the app.

01

Boards

Boards are your top-level workspaces. Create one per project, team, or area of focus. Each board has its own columns, cards, and swimlanes.

  • Create a board from the Dashboard.
  • Give it a name and an optional cover image.
  • Switch between boards via the Dashboard at any time.
02

Columns

Columns represent stages in your workflow — for example: To Do, In Progress, Done. You can add, rename, reorder, and delete columns freely.

  • Add a column using the + Add column button on the top left of the board.
  • Column titles can be renamed by clicking on them to edit.
  • Delete a column from the delete column button in the column header (removes all cards inside it).
03

Cards

Cards are the core unit of work. Each card has a title, description, priority, colour, subtasks, notes log, and AI chat assistant.

  • Add a card with the + Add card button at the top of any column, or by right-clicking the board.
  • Drag cards between columns and swimlanes.
  • Open a card to edit its description, add subtasks, notes, or converse with the AI assistant.
  • Set a priority (1–10) and a colour to help with visual scanning.
04

AI Chat

Each card has its own AI assistant. It has full context of the card — title, description, subtasks, and activity log — so you can ask questions, get summaries, or draft next steps without copy-pasting anything.

  • Open any card and click the AI Chat tab.
  • Type a message and press Enter (or click Send).
  • The AI remembers the conversation history for that card.
  • Use the + button at the top of the chat to start a new conversation, or the clock button to browse chat history.
  • Use the globe button beside the chat input to allow the AI to search the internet when answering.
  • Choose from a wide variety of models in Settings — note that more capable models use your monthly AI budget faster.
💡 The AI assistant reads your card's full context automatically — no need to repeat yourself.
05

Swimlanes

Swimlanes add a horizontal grouping layer across all columns — useful for separating work by team, epic, or priority tier.

  • Add swimlanes from the button at the top left of the board.
  • Delete swimlanes from the swimlane header section.
06

Attachments

You can attach files to any card. Files are stored securely in the cloud.

  • Open a card and go to the attachments button in the submenu.
  • Upload images, PDFs, or documents.
  • Click an attachment to view or download it.
  • If your file contains text, the AI assistant will use it as context when you ask questions.
07

Settings & AI providers

Manage your account, theme, and AI configuration from the Settings page (accessible via the top-right user menu).

  • Theme — choose light, dark, or system default.
  • AI model — pick the specific model you want to use.
  • Account — update your username, email, or password.
  • Billing — manage your plan and subscription.
